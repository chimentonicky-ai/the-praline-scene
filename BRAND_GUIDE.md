# The Praline Scene - Brand Guide

## Brand Personality

- Authentic New Orleans heritage
- Family-owned business
- Handmade in small batches
- Southern hospitality
- Warm, welcoming, nostalgic
- Premium gourmet confectionery
- Elegant but approachable

## Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Soft Blush Pink | #f4d6d8 | Backgrounds, accents |
| Cream | #faf8f5 | Primary background |
| Caramel | #c9944a | CTAs, highlights |
| Chocolate Brown | #5c4033 | Text, navigation |
| Gold | #d4af37 | Premium accents |
| Dark Brown | #3d2817 | Footer, dark text |

## Typography

- **Headlines:** Georgia, Garamond (serif)
- **Body:** System sans-serif (Segoe UI, Roboto, etc.)
- **Sizes:**
  - H1: 3.5rem
  - H2: 2.5rem
  - H3: 2rem
  - Body: 1rem

## Visual Style

- Premium bakery aesthetic
- Warm cinematic lighting
- Shallow depth of field product photography
- Marble surfaces
- Soft pink packaging
- Gold accents
- Cream backgrounds
- High-end product photography
- Luxury bakery atmosphere

## Voice & Tone

- Warm and inviting
- Authentic and genuine
- Knowledgeable but approachable
- Southern charm
- Family-focused
- Quality-conscious

## Key Brand Messages

1. "Authentic New Orleans Pralines Made By Hand"
2. "Small-batch Southern pralines crafted from a cherished family recipe"
3. "Shipped fresh nationwide"
4. "A Family Tradition Worth Sharing"
